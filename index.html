About Us

Meet our Owner Manager: Maureen Okonye. Maureen has been owner-manager of G.H.M Cuisine since 2021. She holds a Diploma in Catering and Hotel Management from the Grano Institute of Catering & Hotel Management and another National Diploma in Mass Communication from Moshood Abiola Polytechnic.

Welcome to G.H.M Cuisine, your trusted partner for commercial catering services, specializing in making your weddings, birthdays, and funeral events unforgettable. With years of experience and a dedication to excellence, we offer personalized catering solutions that ensure your occasion is memorable for all the right reasons. At G.H.M Cuisine, we understand that food plays a central role in every celebration. Whether it’s the joy of a wedding, the heartfelt moments of a birthday, or the solemnity of a funeral, our team is here to provide delicious, thoughtfully prepared meals that reflect the spirit of your event. We are proud to offer a wide variety of catering options that cater to all tastes and dietary preferences. Our restaurant, known for its high-quality dishes and exceptional service, serves as the perfect foundation for our catering business. We believe in using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to deliver food that is not only flavorful but also beautifully presented.
